My Story

With over 15 years of experience in healthcare, I've dedicated my career to providing compassionate, patient-centered care that addresses both mental and physical wellbeing.

My journey began in emergency nursing, where I witnessed firsthand the critical connection between mental health and overall wellness. This inspired me to pursue advanced training in both psychiatric-mental health and family practice.

I believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms. My approach combines evidence-based medicine with genuine listening and personalized treatment plans.

Outside of practice, I'm passionate about mental health advocacy and regularly participate in community education programs to reduce stigma around mental healthcare.
