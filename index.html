About Me

I am Sarah Johnson, a board-certified nurse practitioner with over 15 years of experience in both mental health and family practice. My approach to healthcare combines evidence-based medicine with compassionate, patient-centered care.

I believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms, and work collaboratively with my patients to develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and goals.

My practice is built on trust, respect, and open communication, creating a safe space for healing and growth.
